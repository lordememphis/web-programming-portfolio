Motivation

Well... My motivation is really technology itself (in all its forms), but particularly computer science and software engineering and development. The evidence of what technology can for the people and the world at large is just overwhelming. Even the web browser you're viewing this website from is birthed of technology, and it's just a little over thirty years. Seeing what has been done by developers on the web browser is just as astonishing. It is, therefore, this leverage technology has over most things (if not all things) that marvels me. So, I continue to aspire making more things, with tech (programming), to address the issues of the world, and eventually try to make it a better place. For quite some time now, I've always been enjoying to experiment new technologies, working with people coming from different backgrounds and just being creative. I believe in open-source and collaboration over competition. Moreover, I love sharing my knowledge and experience with others, which is really important if we are to move the world forward.
